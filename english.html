The Starizborn:

Next-generation online performance with innovation, the global stage, and two-way communication along with VR-3D stereoscopic sound technology.

INTERACTIVE

AR x VR x MR = XR; A Stage that connects and communicates with global audiences

By overcoming the limitations of existing face-to-face concerts, where it was difficult to enjoy the performance properly due to the sense of distance between the stage and the audience, global avatar viewers connect the artist and the stage closer together. You will experience a sense of closeness that you could not feel in a face-to-face performance.

IMMERSIVE

Innovative next-generation music content that combines K-culture and 360' ultra-realistic 3D sound technology

By creating a new space through the surround 3D sound effect, we can experience an offline sense of presence only with earphones, providing viewers with a sense of vitality as if they were in the real scene. It is a high-quality digital concert with a level of immersion and quality that could not be realized in existing face-to-face music performances.

INTERNATIONAL

Global stage with viewers and artists from all over the world, including the United States

By overcoming physical limitations such as venue and movement restrictions for face-to-face concerts, it can appeal to the public with high accessibility. Acquiring viewers not only in Korea but also around the world by collaborating with TicTok, which is a live-streaming platform dedicated to music with hundreds of thousands of subscribers.
